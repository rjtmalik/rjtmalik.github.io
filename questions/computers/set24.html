  • What is Pull Technology?Pull technology is a method of obtaining information that relies on a client such as your computer to request a Web page from a server.

  • What is Webcasting ?Webcasting, also called push technology, is a method of obtaining information in which a server automatically downloads content to your computer at regular intervals or whenever updates are made to the site.

  • What is the full form of GUI ?Graphical User Interface

  • What is the full form of PIM ?Personal Information Manager

  • What is the full form of CAD ?Computer-aided Design
