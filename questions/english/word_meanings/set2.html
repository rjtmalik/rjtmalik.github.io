English Word Meanings 2

  • detrimentalTending To cause harm
    It will have detrimental effects on the healing process.

  • renouncereject
    When Hector tried to renounce his membership in the gang, the group’s leader threatened him.

  • renunciateTo Give something away
    The secret of perfect happiness lies in renunciation.

  • digressTo wander in an aimless way
    If you get emotional talking about your father, then you may digress.

  • fabricateTo invent a story or cheat someone
    The journalist got fired when his editor learned of his plan to fabricate a story about the president.

  • heckleTo Harass with questions or taunts
    Some chose to heckle her for her stance

  • ponderousSlow and clumsy becuase of great weight
    Because I like creative articles, I find reading a newspaper to be a ponderous task.
