Light

  • Image formed by the plane mirror is (Always real / Always virtual / Sometimes real sometimes virtual / None of these)Always virtual

  • The light ray, which strikes any surface, is called (Reflected Ray / Normal / Incident Ray / Refracted Ray)Incident Ray

  • The ray that comes back from the surface after reflection is called (Reflected Ray / Normal / Incident Ray / Refracted Ray)Reflected Ray

  • Angle of incidence is( never equal to / always greater than / always smaller than / always equal to ) angle of reflection.Angle of incidence is always equal to angle of reflection.

  • The property of plane mirror to make 'right appear as left' and vice versa, is called ?Lateral inversion

  • We can see ourselves in mirror or polished surface but not on walls because the reflection from walls is ?Irregular reflection

  • (Kaleidoscope / Periscope / Microscope / telescope)can make beautiful patterns due to the formation of multiple images.Kaleidoscope
