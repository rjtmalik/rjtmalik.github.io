About Yourself
  • State your Name
  • Don't say "I am Rajat", say "My name is Rajat".
  • About Your Location
  • Don't say "basically I am from Bihar, but I live in Delhi". Instead, Say "I was born in Biahr but I have been living in Delhi for years".
  • About your family
  • Don't say "I have 2 brothers, sisters and parents". Instead say "We are a family of 5 people which include my parents, a brother, a sister and me."
  • About Education
  • Don't say that "I studied schooling in Patna and college in Delhi". Start in Reverse chronological order. "I did my graduation in Maths from Ramjas college in Delhi University and my schooling from Sachdev School, Patna."
  • About Hobbies
  • Your hobbies should be something you love, not something that you think will look nice on resume. If you don't like to read novels, don't say "Reading" as your hobby.
  • Extra Curricular Activities
  • People are not interested in knowing about the fancy dress competition you participated when you were a toddler. If you have won something in college, you can state that.
