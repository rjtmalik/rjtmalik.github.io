Sound : True/False Type Question

  • Sound is a mechanical wave.True

  • Sound can travel through vacuum.False

  • Sound is produced by a vibrating body.True

  • The maximum displacement of a body from its mean position is called amplitude of oscillation.True

  • Loudness of a sound is measured in Hertz.False(It is Decibel dB)

  • Loudness of a sound depends on the frequency of the sound wave.False(It depends on amplitude)

  • Sound can travel through metals.True

  • In humans, sound is produced by larynx.True

  • Television and transistor radio at high volumes, some kitchen appliances, desert coolers, air conditioners, all contribute to noise pollution.True

  • Plantation on roadside can increase noise pollution.False(Decrease)
