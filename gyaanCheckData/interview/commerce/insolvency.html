Insolvency
  • Definition
  • Insolvent means you have insufficient assets to meet your liabilities.
  • Please List some reasons that can lead to Insolvency.
  • Insolvency can arise from poor cash management, a reduction in the forecasted cash inflow or from an increase in cash expenses.
  • What can insolvency lead to?
  • Insolvency can lead to insolvency proceedings, in which legal action will be taken against the insolvent entity, and assets may be liquidated to pay off outstanding debts.
